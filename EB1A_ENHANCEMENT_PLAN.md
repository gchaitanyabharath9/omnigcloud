## ENHANCEMENT STRATEGY (PER PAPER)

### 1. Original Contribution Section (ADD ONLY)
**Location:** After Abstract, before Introduction  
**Content:**
- Explicit statement of what is novel
- What gap existed in prior work
- Why prior approaches were insufficient
- Use cautious academic phrasing ("to the best of our knowledge")

**Estimated Addition:** +200-300 words per paper

### 2. Independent Authorship Clarification (ADD ONLY)
**Location:** In Introduction or as footnote  
**Content:**
- "This work was developed independently and is not proprietary to any employer or commercial entity."
- Clarify that research is original and not derivative

**Estimated Addition:** +50-100 words per paper

### 3. Practical and Scholarly Impact Section (ADD ONLY)
**Location:** Before Conclusion  
**Content:**
- Who can use this work
- How it influences decision-making
- Why it matters beyond one organization
- Bridge between theory and practice

**Estimated Addition:** +300-400 words per paper

### 4. Limitations / Threats to Validity Section (ADD ONLY)
**Location:** Before Conclusion  
**Content:**
- Mature, academic limitations discussion
- Threats to internal/external validity
- Boundary conditions
- Future work to address limitations

**Estimated Addition:** +250-350 words per paper

### 5. Diagram Explanation Expansion (INLINE EXPANSION)
**Location:** Around each diagram  
**Content:**
- Expand surrounding text to explain control flow
- Detail feedback loops
- Clarify policy reasoning
- Expand captions instead of shortening text

**Estimated Addition:** +100-200 words per diagram (varies by paper)
