# INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY PROTECTION & BRAND STRATEGY

---

## 1. Recommendations for Protecting Technical Artifacts

### 1.1 PDFs & Whitepapers
- **Watermark Strategy**: Apply a subtle, transparent watermark: "© [Applicant Name] 2024 - EB-1A Evidence Exhibit" across all pages.
- **Footer Attribution**: Every page must contain: "Technical work independently authored and implemented by [Applicant Name]. All rights reserved."
- **Metadata Tagging**: Ensure PDF metadata (properties) lists [Applicant Name] as the 'Author' and contains the document title.
- **Read-Only Export**: Distribute only as flattened PDFs to prevent text extraction and unauthorized modification.
- **Versioning**: Always include a version (e.g., v1.0, v1.1) and a publication date to establish a clear timeline of original work.

### 1.2 PPTs & Diagrams
- **Attribution Footers**: Include the author's name on every slide.
- **Slide Numbering + Exhibit IDs**: Clearly label each slide as an exhibit (e.g., Exhibit B-1) to ensure legal references are unambiguous.
- **Export-to-PDF**: For public sharing or submission, always export to PDF. Never share the original .pptx or .drawio files.

### 1.3 Website Content
- **Copyright Notice**: Include a prominent copyright notice in the site footer: "© 2024 OmniGCloud. Technical architecture and research artifacts by [Applicant Name]. All rights reserved."
- **Layered Visibility**: Publicly disseminate the *methodology* (the "how" and "why") on `/research`, but keep the *implementation logic* (the proprietary source code) in private repositories.
- **Controlled Access**: For highly sensitive metrics or proprietary architecture diagrams, consider requiring a light "Register to Download" wall to track interest while maintaining public accessibility.

## 2. Marketing-Safe Language Examples (Layer 1)

| Context | Avoid (High-Risk) | Use (Safe & Credible) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **New Features** | "The world's first i18n checker" | "Implements automated i18n safeguards" |
| **Technology** | "We invented a new framework" | "Independently designed a proprietary framework" |
| **Impact** | "A revolutionary way to test" | "Addresses previously unmitigated risks in global QA" |
| **Adoption** | "The new industry standard" | "Demonstrates non-trivial engineering effort at scale" |
| **Methodology** | "Only we have this technology"| "Built on a research-backed architecture" |
