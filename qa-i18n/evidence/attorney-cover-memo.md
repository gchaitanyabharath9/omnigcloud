## 1. Overview of the Contribution
The Applicant has independently designed and implemented an original **Automated Multilingual Quality Assurance (i18n QA) Framework**. This software proactively solves a critical technical risk in global software delivery: "Silent Regressions." These are hidden defects where missing translated content is masked by automated English-language fail-safes (fallbacks). The Framework utilizes an original discovery engine to automate the verification of a platform's entire multi-language surface area.

## 3. Satisfaction of EB-1A Regulatory Criteria

### Original Contribution Criterion
The Framework represents an original implementation that extends beyond the capabilities of standard industry tools. The design of a "Tiered Gating Policy" combined with a custom-engineered request interceptor represents an independent engineering effort to solve a previously unmitigated technical risk. (Reference: **Exhibit B**).

### Major Significance Criterion
The system serves as a mandatory quality-control gate that has demonstrated measurable operational impact. By replacing approximately 35 hours of manual verification with a reliable 4-minute automated process, the contribution has significantly improved software reliability across eight international markets. (Reference: **Exhibit C**).

## 4. Closing Statement
The transition from manual, error-prone language verification to a deterministic, automated gating system represents an original engineering achievement. This work is both original in its design and of major significance to the field of software architecture.
